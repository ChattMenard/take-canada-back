### Pattern Analysis
- **Cross-Partisan Donations:** Pharma companies donated to Conservative, Liberal, and Bloc Québécois parties
- **Timing:** Donations concentrated in 2004-2006 period (pre-2007 corporate donation restrictions)
- **Amounts:** Individual donations ranged from $185 to $2,000
- **Geographic Spread:** Donations to riding associations across Canada (QC, ON, AB)

### Key Observations
1. **No Modern Data:** Current search only captured 2004-2006. Need to filter for 2015-2025 (COVID/pandemic period)
2. **Corporate Ban:** Federal corporate donations banned in 2015. Modern donations would be from individuals/executives
3. **Cross-Party Influence:** Pharma maintained relationships with all major parties
