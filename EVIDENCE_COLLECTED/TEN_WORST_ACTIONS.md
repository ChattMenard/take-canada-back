## 1. SECRET VACCINE CONTRACTS WITH PHARMA

**What happened:**
- Signed $5-9B in vaccine contracts with Pfizer and Moderna
- Contract terms, pricing, and liability provisions kept completely secret from Canadians
- No parliamentary oversight or public disclosure

**Evidence:**
- Contracts referenced in parliamentary questions but contents redacted
- Auditor General confirmed $1B in vaccine wastage due to over-purchasing
- No public access to pricing per dose or liability terms

**Harm:**
- Canadians forced to pay for vaccines without knowing the cost
- Pharma granted liability immunity (if harmed by vaccine, no recourse)
- $1B wasted while healthcare was underfunded
- Set precedent that government can sign massive secret contracts with corporations

**Who benefited:** Pfizer, Moderna, government officials (future pharma board positions)
**Who paid:** Canadian taxpayers

**Evidence:**

**Harm:**
- Canadians continued paying inflated drug prices
- PMPRB independence destroyed
- Pharma profits protected at expense of sick Canadians
- Set precedent that ministers can override independent regulators

**Who benefited:** Pfizer, Merck, GSK, all IMC member companies
**Who paid:** Canadian patients (higher drug costs)
