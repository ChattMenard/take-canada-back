## 🎯 ATIP REQUEST 1: PFIZER VACCINE CONTRACT

### Request Details
- **Institution:** Public Services and Procurement Canada (PSPC)
- **Subject:** Full Pfizer COVID-19 vaccine contract terms and pricing
- **Time Period:** 2020-2025

### Request Text
```
I am requesting access to the following records under the Access to Information Act:

1. The complete contract between the Government of Canada and Pfizer Inc. for the procurement of COVID-19 vaccines, including all amendments, schedules, and annexes.

2. Specifically, I am requesting:
   - Full contract terms and conditions
   - Pricing per dose (including any redacted information with justification for redaction)
   - Liability and indemnification provisions
   - Delivery schedules and penalties
   - Total contract value and payment terms
   - Any side agreements or memoranda of understanding

3. All internal communications, emails, briefing notes, and memoranda related to the negotiation and signing of this contract.

4. All risk assessments, due diligence reports, and financial analyses prepared in relation to this contract.

Please apply the Access to Information Act's presumption of disclosure. If any information is withheld, please provide specific section-by-section justifications for each redaction.
```

### Priority Information
- Contract pricing (currently confidential)
- Liability provisions
- Total contract value
- Amendment history

## 🎯 SUBMISSION INSTRUCTIONS

### How to Submit
1. **Online Portal:** Submit via the Government of Canada ATIP Online Request Portal
2. **Fee:** $5 application fee per request (can be waived for financial hardship)
3. **Processing Time:** 30 days statutory response time (may be extended)
4. **Format:** Submit each request separately to different institutions

### Follow-Up Actions
- Track request status online
- File complaints if response is incomplete or delayed
- Request review by Information Commissioner if denied
- Publish received documents to evidence repository

### Coordination with Parliamentary Questions
- These ATIP requests complement parliamentary questions Q-735, Q-736, Q-603
- Use parliamentary question responses to refine ATIP requests
- Cross-reference ATIP results with parliamentary scrutiny
