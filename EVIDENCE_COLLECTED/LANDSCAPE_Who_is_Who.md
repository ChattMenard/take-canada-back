## HOW TO USE THIS DIRECTORY

### When You Need to Explain a Connection

Example:
> "How did the pharmaceutical industry capture Health Canada?"

Answer:
> "Through the revolving door. Michel Fralick was a senior Health Canada official from 2008–2019. He left and became VP of Innovative Medicines Canada — the trade association for Pfizer, J&J, AstraZeneca, and Merck. Now he lobbies his former colleagues. That's not just a job change. It's regulatory capture."

### When You Need to Show Institutional Relationships

Example:
> "Who funded the mRNA platform before COVID?"

Answer:
> "Multiple government agencies: DARPA funded the LNP delivery system through its ADEPT program. BARDA invested over $500 million in manufacturing capacity. NIAID funded Moderna's research from 2013. And CEPI — founded in 2017 with $460 million from the Gates Foundation, Wellcome Trust, and governments — was explicitly created to fund 'rapid vaccine development platforms.'"

### When You Need to Show the Secrecy Was Global

Example:
> "Was Canada the only country hiding vaccine information?"

Answer:
> "No. The FDA redacted ingredients in the U.S. The European Commission hid its Pfizer contract until it was leaked by Italian media. The UK government redacted its contract and the Information Commissioner upheld the redaction. Canada used confidentiality clauses. This was not one government's mistake. It was a **coordinated global secrecy architecture**."
