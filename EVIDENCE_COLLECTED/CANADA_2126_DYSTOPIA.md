- **Housing segregation** - Platinum zones, Gold zones, Bronze zones
- **Education privatized** - Only Platinum children attend real schools
- **Healthcare tiered** - Platinum: immediate care; Gold: 30-day wait; Bronze: emergency only
- **Movement restricted** - Bronze require permits to enter Gold zones

- **AI governance** - All decisions made by Brookfield algorithm

- **Credit scores mandatory** - Bronze citizens with low scores denied services
- **Reproduction regulated** - Bronze require license to have children
- **Work mandatory** - Bronze citizens work 60 hours/week for housing credits

### 2070-2080: Resource Extraction

- **Water privatized** - Brookfield owns all water rights
- **Air quality taxed** - Bronze citizens pay for clean air
- **Climate refugees accepted** - As cheap labor force
- **Indigenous lands seized** - "National security" designation

### 2080-2090: Collapse Phase
- **Infrastructure crumbling** - Only Platinum zones maintained
- **Famine in Bronze zones** - Food rationing implemented
- **Plague sweeps Bronze zones** - Healthcare denied
- **Uprisings crushed** - Private security forces (Intelcom) suppress dissent
- **Mass exodus** - 5M citizens flee to US (now also corporate state)

### 2090-2100: Final Consolidation
- **Canada renamed** - "North American Resource Zone, Sector 7"
- **Government dissolved** - Corporate board replaces parliament
- **Citizenship abolished** - All residents are "employees"
- **Constitution burned** - No rights, only contracts
- **Population reduced** - From 40M to 15M (unprofitable eliminated)
