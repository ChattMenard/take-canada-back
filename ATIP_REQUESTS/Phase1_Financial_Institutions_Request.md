## Request Details

Pursuant to Section 4(1) of the Access to Information Act, I request the following records:

### 1. Investment Portfolio Disclosures
All investment portfolio disclosures for government officials, including:

- Stock and bond holdings by company and sector
- Mutual fund and ETF investments with fund details
- Real estate investment trusts (REITs) and property holdings
- Private equity and venture capital investments
- Commodity and precious metals holdings
- Foreign investments and international securities
- Derivative and option positions
- Cash and money market holdings

### 2. Trust and Estate Planning Documents
All records related to:

- Family trust structures and arrangements
- Trust documents and declarations
- Estate planning wills and testaments
- Beneficiary designations and entitlements
- Wealth transfer mechanisms and timing
- Power of attorney arrangements
- Guardianship and custodial accounts
- Inter-generational transfer planning

### 3. Financial Account Information
All records related to:

- Investment accounts and brokerage services
- Private banking relationships and services
- Wealth management accounts and portfolios
- Family office arrangements and services
- Trust company accounts and administration
- Offshore accounts and international holdings
- Corporate accounts and business investments
- Joint accounts and shared holdings

### 4. Compliance and Conflict Management Records
All records related to:

- Blind trust establishment and monitoring
- Conflict of interest assessments and reviews
- Ethics compliance documentation and reports
- Enforcement actions and remedial measures
- Compliance monitoring and reporting
- Violation investigations and findings
- Penalty assessments and collections
- Ongoing compliance requirements
