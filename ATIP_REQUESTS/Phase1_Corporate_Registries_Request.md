## Request Details

Pursuant to Section 4(1) of the Access to Information Act, I request the following records:

### 1. Corporate Registry Records
All corporate registry records for businesses owned or directed by:

- Current Cabinet ministers and their spouses
- Senior government officials and their family members
- Former ministers who left office after January 1, 2015
- Family members of current and former officials

Including:

- Company incorporation documents and articles of incorporation
- Business registration and licensing records
- Corporate bylaws and governance documents
- Shareholder registers and ownership structures
- Director and officer appointments and resignations
- Annual corporate returns and financial statements
- Business activity descriptions and sector classifications

### 2. Business Establishment Records
All records related to:

- Company establishment dates and incorporation processes
- Initial capitalization and share structures
- Business registration numbers and identifiers
- Principal business activities and operations
- Registered office addresses and contact information
- Business licenses and permits required
- Professional registrations and certifications

### 3. Director and Officer Information
All records related to:

- Director appointments and terms of service
- Officer positions and responsibilities
- Director remuneration and compensation
- Board meeting attendance and participation
- Related party transactions and disclosures
- Conflict of interest declarations
- Professional qualifications and experience

### 4. Investment Portfolio and Trust Records
All records related to:

- Family office structures and operations
- Investment fund holdings and management
- Blind trust arrangements and compliance
- Inter-generational wealth transfer structures
- Trust documents and beneficiary information
- Estate planning arrangements and wills
- Wealth transfer mechanisms and timing
