## Request Details

Pursuant to Section 4(1) of the Access to Information Act, I request the following records:

### 1. Cabinet Meeting Minutes
All Cabinet meeting minutes from January 1, 2015 to present, including:

- Full meeting minutes and agendas
- Policy discussion topics and summaries
- Decision-making processes and outcomes
- Ministerial positions and arguments presented
- Voting records and consensus decisions
- Implementation timelines and action items
- Follow-up requirements and reporting

### 2. Treasury Board Submissions
All Treasury Board submissions related to:

- New spending programs and initiatives
- Major procurement projects and contracts
- Regulatory changes and implementations
- Tax policy changes and revenue measures
- Infrastructure investments and projects
- Program evaluations and reviews
- Risk assessments and mitigation strategies

### 3. Policy Development Records
All records regarding policy development for:

- Economic policy and fiscal measures
- Housing policy and affordability initiatives
- Infrastructure spending and development
- Environmental regulations and climate policies
- Trade agreements and international commerce
- Health policy and pharmaceutical regulations
- Transportation policy and infrastructure

### 4. Ministerial Communications
All communications regarding policy decisions with:

- External stakeholders and industry groups
- Provincial and territorial governments
- International partners and organizations
- Public consultations and feedback processes
- Expert advisory panels and committees
- Think tanks and policy research organizations
