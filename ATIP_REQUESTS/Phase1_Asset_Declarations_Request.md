## Request Details

Pursuant to Section 4(1) of the Access to Information Act, I request the following records:

### 1. Asset Declarations
All asset declarations filed by Cabinet ministers from January 1, 2015 to present, including:

- Initial declarations upon appointment to Cabinet
- Annual updates and amendments
- Spouse and dependent asset disclosures
- Business interest declarations
- Real estate holdings
- Investment portfolios and holdings
- Foreign assets and interests
- Debt and liability disclosures

### 2. Conflict of Interest Assessments
All conflict of interest assessments conducted for ministers regarding:

- Specific policy decisions under their responsibility
- Cabinet discussions and decisions
- Departmental matters and initiatives
- Regulatory changes and implementations
- Procurement decisions and contract awards
- International trade missions and agreements
- Emergency measures and crisis responses

### 3. Compliance and Enforcement Records
All records related to:

- Complaints received about potential conflicts of interest
- Investigations initiated and conducted
- Findings, determinations, and outcomes
- Recommendations made and implemented
- Enforcement actions taken
- Remedial measures required
- Penalties or sanctions imposed

### 4. Communications Records
All communications between the Commissioner and ministerial offices regarding:

- Potential conflicts of interest
- Compliance requirements and obligations
- Asset management guidance
- Ethical obligations and standards
- Interpretation of conflict rules
- Pre-clearance requests and approvals
- Ongoing monitoring and reporting

## Public Interest Justification

This request relates to a matter of significant public interest — understanding how government officials manage potential conflicts between public duties and private interests. The requested information will:

- Contribute to democratic oversight of government officials
