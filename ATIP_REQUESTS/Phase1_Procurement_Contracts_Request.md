## Request Details

Pursuant to Section 4(1) of the Access to Information Act, I request the following records:

### 1. Contract Awards Over $100,000
All government contract awards from January 1, 2015 to present, including:

- Contract details and specifications
- Award criteria and evaluation process
- Bidding process documentation
- Contractor information and ownership structure
- Contract value and payment terms
- Performance requirements and deliverables
- Contract amendments and extensions
- Completion reports and evaluations

### 2. Sole-Source and Emergency Procurements
All non-competitive procurement contracts, including:

- Justification for sole-source awards
- Emergency procurement declarations
- Market research conducted
- Price reasonableness assessments
- Supplier pre-qualification processes
- Limited tendering documentation
- Direct award justifications

### 3. Contractor Information and Ownership
All records related to contractor ownership and connections, including:

- Corporate ownership structures
- Director and officer information
- Shareholder details and affiliations
- Political connections and donations
- Previous government contracts
- Subcontractor arrangements
- Joint venture partnerships

### 4. Procurement Decision Records
All records related to procurement decision-making, including:

- Evaluation committee memberships
- Scoring criteria and weightings
- Technical and financial evaluations
- Risk assessments and mitigations
- Due diligence investigations
- Conflict of interest assessments
- Approval documentation and sign-offs
