## **📊 PUBLIC DATABASES READY FOR ACCESS:**

### 1. **Elections Canada Political Donations**
**Database:** Federal political contributions
**URL:** https://www.elections.ca/content.aspx?section=fin&dir=rep&document=index&lang=e
**Evidence Needed:**
- Political donations by corporations and individuals
- Donations to ministers and their ridings
- Timing of donations vs policy decisions
- Donation patterns from connected entities

**Collection Method:**
- Download contribution reports by riding and party
- Search for donations from known corporations
- Identify donations from government contractors
- Track donation timing patterns

### 2. **Office of the Lobbying Commissioner**
**Database:** Lobbying registration records
**URL:** https://clrs-sprc.search.open.canada.ca/en/
**Evidence Needed:**
- Lobbying registrations by corporations
- Communications with government officials
- Policy discussions and consultations
- Revolving door employment patterns

**Collection Method:**
- Search lobbying registrations by corporation name
- Filter by government department and official
- Download communication reports
- Track lobbying timing vs policy changes

### 3. **Provincial Corporate Registries**
**Database:** Business incorporation and ownership records
**Evidence Needed:**
- Corporate directorships of officials/family members
- Business establishment dates
- Ownership structures and shareholders
- Corporate dissolution and changes

**Collection Method:**
- Search registries by official and family member names
- Track business establishment dates
- Document ownership structures
- Identify policy-sensitive sectors

### 4. **Land Registry Offices**
**Database:** Property ownership and transactions
**Evidence Needed:**
- Real estate transactions by officials/family
- Property value changes over time
- Land banking activities
- Geographic patterns of investments

**Collection Method:**
- Search property records by official names
- Track transaction dates and values
- Identify investment patterns
- Map property locations to policy impacts

### 5. **Securities Commission Filings**
**Database:** Public company disclosures and insider trading
**Evidence Needed:**
- Insider trading reports
- Stock ownership in public companies
- Option grants and exercises
- Beneficial ownership disclosures

**Collection Method:**
- Search insider trading reports by official names
- Track stock transactions timing
- Identify policy-sensitive investments
- Document option exercises
